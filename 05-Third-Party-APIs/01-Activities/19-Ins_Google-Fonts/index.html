Typography with Google Fonts

A quick demo showing how to extend Bootstrap with custom fonts and styles.

Clean and Readable

A modern byline using lighter weights

This page uses Titillium Web—a versatile Google Font known for its clarity and modern appeal. Notice how Bootstrap’s default typography has been softened with custom CSS variables and spacing utilities.

You can easily switch to another Google Font by changing the link tag in the head and updating the font-family in your stylesheet.
